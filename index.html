3.  Project Description

3.1.                     Overview

3.1.1.    Topic

The project is named Melody Valley, which tells its destiny of making a heavily theory-based subject like History become as joyful as listening to a melody - our aim to make Vietnamese History fascinating and easy to learn. The gameplay experience and its lessons will be smoothly blended together to uphold the sense of excitement while acquiring new knowledge. The game promises to deliver entertainment and education values to players around the world. Players can challenge themselves by trying to answer historical questions while playing the game and receive rewards for every correct answer.

Within this Melody Valley, players can choose to fight with or against each other, complete basic quests or daily missions, accumulate money to trade items, have pets, purchase skins & outfits, etc. Players can also socialize (i.e., add friends, chat, talk, share daily thoughts) in the game. Additionally, the main engaging characteristics of this project is its immersion, competitiveness and the sense of achievement nature. Unlike real-time strategy games, our MMO game emphasizes on role-playing characters, so that players can immerse in a fictional world with their own customizable characters.

3.1.2.   Motivation

According to mmo-population.com, in 2015, the game World of Warcraft alone has the total population of about 13 million players. Then, as of 2020, after 5 years, there are 89 million players, about 685% up from 2015. And 2021 has already witnessed a population of 115 million players in August. This game, which is increasing in popularity, is of MMO type. MMO stands for Massively Multiplayer Online. The prominent characteristic of this gameplay is its immense scope, which involves thousands or millions of players who coexist and interact with each other in the server. MMO games are prospering.

With this project, we want to bring more values to customers other than just aiming for monetization purposes. This project benefits us all both ways. For client’s side, our project brings them entertainment and stress-release values. To us as creators, we got to learn our first project and understand the competitors in the field. In today’s world where stress is increasing, game brings more than just entertainment values for gamers and profits for creators. Game actually has the ability to reduce stress and depression. Moreover, we want to create story with moral and encouraging values. Throughout the years, MMORPGS have become the most popular type of online game. Furthermore, according to Mordorintelligence, many factors are driving the growth of MMORPG such as the increasing use of the internet and COVID-19 pandemic, especially in Vietnam.

Figure 1. An illustrate of MMORPG Gaming Market by Geography (2020 – 2025)

 

3.1.3.   Landscape

Some of our possible competitors can be Laplace M, Genshin Impact, which are all well-known MMORPG games. However, what distinguishes our game project with the rest of existing competitors in the market is education. We want to make the game a knowledge companion for all players where they can acquire new knowledge and soft skills and through events and quests in a fun way. Specifically, with English as our main platform’s language, we want to leverage the storyline and make use of as much as academic words to help players learn while traversing through various chapters. At the same time, we also want to incorporate historical elements in the storyline to help users understand more about the Vietnamese history.

3.2.                     Detailed Description

3.2.1.   Aim

The mission of our game project is to make Vietnamese History become more fascinating and pleasant to learn for the young generation and stakeholders from around the world. The biggest lesson one can learn from our historic victories despite the significant disparity of military resources is that our people always unite and look in the same direction, or as one saying in Vietnamese “Lá lành đùm lá rách” - the good leaves cover the torn ones, and we care for each other. Especially in this time of conflict when the COVID pandemic hits hard and when everybody is in the constant fear of lost and distrust, the importance of building trust, love, and eventually union in the heart of the nation’s people should be focused on more than ever. There are numerous traditional values that can be honored and learned from our recorded history. With our game, we want to smoothly bring these values to our players’ gameplay experiences and help them learn while enjoying the fun parts of MMORPG games. Furthermore, as there are numerous distorted sources of information about the Vietnamese History around the world, we want to let players and stakeholders from around the world have a closer approach to the truth of the History by using English as the platform’s main language.

3.2.2.    Goals

3.2.2.1.            History-based Storyline

Our goal is to build up a storyline which is imbued with the historical elements while conserving the colors of MMORPG games. Players will have fantasy characters of their choice and these characters will become a part of the history flow in the storyline. There are choices to make, and each choice brings about different outcomes. For example, having a female character, in the Hung King Dynasty story chapter, players will have to choose to marry either Son Tinh – the Mountain God or Thuy Tinh – The Sea God. Having the right to choose the outcome of the story, players could manage to answer some of the question in the traditional history class: What would happen if this man did things the other way? Why should the king ask the intellectuals? Thus, the players’ gaming experience will become more immersive, and they can better comprehend the insights of History.

3.2.2.2.            Quiz

To make the game more exciting, our goal in this project is to make small challenges of the Vietnamese historical knowledge for players and reward those who could provide the correct answers. This could come in many forms such as quizzes before fighting a boss dungeon (reward: increasing attack points/attack speed/attack range, etc.), multiple choice questions in special events (reward: increase EXP or receive normal items) and so forth.

3.2.2.3.            Forum for history discussion

In order to create a strong community where all members have a vested interest in the Vietnamese history, we have built a goal to create a forum to discuss difficult historical questions which arise from exploring the history, storyline, and quizzes. In that way, members can gain and spread their knowledge to others and their experiences with the game can become more joyful.

3.3.1.    Initial idea

In the first days, our aim was solely intended for the enjoyment of players who are interested in the fantasy world. To achieve such aim, we have tested in made an extensive amount of research about different game types in the market to choose the most suitable ones. Among the best candidates, MMORPGs game seems to be the most ideal game type because it lets players fight with and against each other or monsters whilst enjoying the medieval context. Therefore, we have decided to build a MMORPGs game to cater to players who seek for entertainment. This humble aim, in our opinions, is appropriate and plausible to our limited knowledge as fresher IT students. However, realizing our thirst for new knowledge and desire to bring more values to customers, we agreed to expand the aim of the project to the education field. In addition to that, we have encountered one dead-end while developing this idea, which is our competitors. In fact, there are countless number of competitors in the marketplace who produce MMORPG games. Therefore, we have decided to avoid as much as competition by diving deep into niche edge of the market, bringing more values to the players and serve a more specific user group which includes the Vietnamese students and foreigners who have interest in the history of Vietnam. By doing so, we can simultaneously provide players the education, entertainment values as well as have a chance to compete in the marketplace.

3.5.                     Scope and Limits

3.5.1.    In-scope features

3.5.1.1.            Immersion

Researchers identified immersion as one of the engaging factors of games (Nordin, 2014) The concept of immersive gameplay experience in the game industry can be defined as the experience to be drifted the game’s inner world, or “being in the game”. This factor is important in engrossing player as it keep them dedicated and motivate them to return, which is vital to game enjoyment.

The following figure illustrate the relation of factors involving in Player Engagement Process (Henrik Schoenau-Fog). We will be designing the game based on this framework.

Figure 2. The OA3 framework of relations between elements of Player Engagement Process

We believe immersion is important to both the psychological attractions of the game but also the mission of the game, which is about smoothly brings the Vietnam history flow to players’ mindset. This could be achieved by allowing players to make their own choices that ultimately affect the outcomes and the result of the game. Unpredictable outcomes will trigger players to explore more and understand more of the event of history.

3.5.1.2.            Interactivity

Interactivity can facilitate the immersive gameplay experience (Tanskaken, 2018). There are two types of interactions in games including Players vs Environment (PvE) and Players vs Players (PvP).

o   PVE: players will battle with monsters and final bosses to gain experience point. In addition to defeating these bosses and monsters, there is a percentage for the players to pick up dropped mythical equipment which can tremendously improve the character’s power. Received gears will be different depending on the class of the character, but the power statistics of the gears will be the same for characters of all classes, having the same level and doing the same quest. To enhance excitement, randomly spawning enemies will appear on the players’ journey of conquering quests. Moreover, some event-only bosses whose attack points and health points are extremely high will be added so as to challenge players and require them to cooperate with others to defeat these bosses and get valuable items.

o   PVP: Players’ characters may have different privileges and power, but all are eligible to join these PVP (player versus player) fights. This feature allows players to compete with each other to reflect how effective their characters’ training strategy is. In addition, players whose strength are less than the rivals still have a chance of victory if they can show off their mastery of playstyle and skillsets.

o  

3.5.1.3.            Character Classes and Appearance

Classes affect the playstyle, skillsets, and power scale of a character. For instance, a melee class character whose attack range is small typically possesses more attack power than a wizard class character whose attack range is bigger. Depending on players’ forte, they are allowed to have their own choice of characters’ class at the beginning of the game (i.e., this feature will appear immediately after the user login session to rest assured that players have at least one chosen character of their account). Players can also choose for themselves the initial characters’ name, gender, appearance (including hair, beard (for male characters), eyebrows, body, etc.), voice (e.g., soft, strong, neutral-soft, neutral, etc.) and many more customizations to make the characters more appealing. Afterwards, they cannot change the characters’ classes until they have met the game requirements at certain levels.

3.5.1.4.            Game Currency

Currency is used to trade items in the market. The game will have 4 types of currency:

§  ♪ : all players can achieve this by completing as much standard quests as they can. This coin is used for upgrading equipment.

§  ♫: This coin is used for buying items in the store. In addition, ♫ can be convert to ♪ with one-to-one ratio, but ♪ cannot be converted back to ♫.

§  ♬: This currency is particularly challenging to acquire since it is used for trading special treasures and rare items & equipment. In order to possess this coin, players have to reach high achievements and rankings in an event.

§  : This currency is only available for in-game purchase (with their money). Players who have this currency can use it to buy exclusive items from the game creators.

3.5.1.5.            Social Hub

Social Hub basically allows players to connect, chat and talk with each other like other social media platforms. We will incorporate personal chatting, mailing system, and world channel features to our social hub in order to enhance players’ in-game interactions:  

§  Personal Chatting: Players can make friends to be able to chat with others. Depending on their preferences, a chat box can include as many members as they want, as long as it is between 2 (at least) and 5 (at most).

§  Mailing System: This feature is primarily for the game publishers’ announcement, event & quest gifts/rewards. Furthermore, items from successful trading in the Black Market will be sent directly to the recipients’ mails.

§  World channel: This channel is for communication among all players no matter their friendship status, privileges, and strength. To prevent spamming and scamming, this channel is restricted to those who possess the World Orator item which can be purchased using the ♬ coin.

Additionally, we are going to develop relationship system that can stack their friendship points through their co-participations when players perform some activities together. There will be some milestones for them to achieve, and each milestone they reach will help them unlock some special abilities.

3.5.1.6.            Quest

Quest is the mission player need to accomplish in order to earn currency and equipment. This feature allows players to get rare equipment and gain a significant amount of experience point when the assigned quest is accomplished. There are a diverse set of quests which ranges from collecting required items, hunting bosses & monsters, survival mode, making friends, joining a class, etc. Therefore, players always have different missions of all difficulty levels to try out.

3.5.1.7.            Black Market

A trading system that will allow players to trade items and materials. Players can also sell items such as weapons or outfits in exchange for the ♬ coins. For the rare and mythical items/weapons, they can perform bidding sessions, so the player who bid with the highest price can achieve it.

3.5.1.8.      Outfits & Skins

To enhance the appearance of character, these features will let players choose all skins available to their character’s class. Some of the skins will be offered for free in some events, but some are exclusive and only available through real purchases (for example, the Wizard Prestige Edition is a special skin which is only available for those who make in-game purchase)

3.5.1.9.            Character Evolution

Through out the course of the training process, when characters reach certain points of strength or level, they will undergo the evolution. Post evolutions, each of the characters’ power statistics such as attack, speed, health, stamina, etc., will be incremented by certain points and the appearance of the characters will be changed. This feature is promising to delivering players a sense of achievement in the game.

3.5.1.10.        Enchantment

Players can upgrade their weapons to make their characters stronger using required ingredients. Each level needs different items and certain amount of ♪ coins, but it is not guaranteed that the weapons can be 100% leveled up. In fact, we will restrict the success rate of the upgrade to be less than its previous level. For example, level 1: 100%; level 2: 95%; level 3: 90%; level 4: 80%; etc.).

3.5.2.    Out of Scope

The following list comprises of some features we want to include in the game. However, they are not available for this project since some of them are hardly attainable within given time slots or not so important:

3.5.2.1.            Farm

Farm is considered gamer-own territory in the game where they can grow plants and make products to complete the orders. Players will get exp and farm coins which use to buy decorations for the farm in the grocery.

The players can decorate their own farm.

 

3.5.2.2.            Marriage System

The marriage system will also tighten the relationships between players. At the same time, it also increases their power and unlock some exclusive skills that normal players cannot possess. When time allows, we will design an in-game wedding venue for couples to get married and a lobby for other players to attend the wedding.

3.5.2.3.            Riding Pet/Mount

Pets and Mounts are indeed some of the most indispensable elements in any MMORPG game as they provide players a sense of having companions. However, since 16 week is a short period of time for any MMORPG game developer, the project’s scope cannot include this feature.

3.5.2.4.            Profanity Filter

Acknowledging cyber bullying issues of many games in the marketplace, we would like to tackle them by filtering out inappropriate words, signs of racism and so on in all of our chat systems and strictly banning all players who make serious violations.

Post the 16-week development period, if the game reaches or exceed its 5000 players milestone, we will consider implementing these extra set of features to our platform so as to augment our gamers’ experience.

This project is indeed challenging for our team as all members are first-year students in RMIT. Thus, the lack of knowledge and experience may come at a heavy cost, especially when dealing with the lack of support of some required technologies under a narrow and restricted timeframe.

In addition, disasters and weather-related adversities may prevent the group members from submitting the assignment in many possible ways such as electricity cut-offs, family issues, etc.

3.9.3.      Time management

The time-consuming process of making game projects may lead to time management issues because there will be some features whose difficulties are underestimated until our group starts to do them. As a result, some tasks may take unexpectedly long hours to be accomplished.

3.9.4.      Interpersonal Problem

This is the very first team collaboration of each member of our group. Therefore, some group members may not understand each other well enough, and therefore, the project workflow may be hindered. If the communication and collaboration issues is unable to be resolved, the project outcome will be in low-quality, and the time taken to improve it will be more costly.

3.10.                Group Processes and Communication

Since this course is fully conducted online, we all communicate via social medias to simultaneously get to know each other and boost up the productivity. Therefore, we mainly contact each other through Facebook Messenger to discuss some basic ideas, make announcements, ask questions, etc. Moreover, there are technical ideas relevant to our project that cannot be effectively expressed in written communication, so we use the Discord platform to communicate since it is optimized for group voice chatting. During project proposal, we discuss about our project daily, perform voice meetings 2-3 times a week and use Microsoft Word Online as our main platform for written collaborations. In addition, each member is subjected to collaborate with each other daily at 8 p.m. to work on their assigned tasks. Since the minimum time required for each member’s work session is 1 hour, we can ensure that all members have spent consistent and sufficient dedications to the project and the workload is fully under control. In case when someone is unable to work under the specified time, we will ask them to work extra hours to offset missing sessions. Any member who rarely responds to our discussion should explain their reasons of absence, and we will take note for their participations if they keep the same behaviour as before.
